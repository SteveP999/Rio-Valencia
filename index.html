About

Rio Valencia

Rio Valencia doesn't pick a lane — he drives through all of them. Born where two worlds meet, his sound moves between English and Spanish the way a city moves between day and night: naturally, without warning, and with something electric in the air.

His debut album Midnight Line is a cinematic journey through late-night streets and long-distance feelings — bilingual storytelling wrapped in sleek Latin pop production with a pulse that crosses every border.

Released through Hello Texas Records. No limits. No translation required.
